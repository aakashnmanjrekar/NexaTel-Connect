Frequently asked questions
›

When is my monthly payment due?

Your invoice is generated on the 1st of each month and payment is due within 14 days. You'll receive a reminder by SMS and email before the due date.

›

What happens if I exceed my data limit?

Once you reach your monthly data allowance, additional data is charged at your plan's standard overage rate. You can top up anytime from your account.

›

How do I change or upgrade my plan?

Plan changes take effect from your next billing cycle. Contact us and we'll walk you through the available options for your account.

›

What should I do if I lose my SIM card?

Report a lost or stolen SIM immediately so we can block it and protect your account. A replacement SIM will be dispatched within 2–3 business days.

›

Are there charges for calls made abroad?

International and roaming charges vary by destination and are billed separately from your standard monthly plan. Check your plan details for applicable rates.
